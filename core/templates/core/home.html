What We Do

Five Pillars of SHE Report

An integrated ecosystem addressing every dimension of crimes against women in Kerala

📊

Data Curation & Mapping

Daily systematic curation from police, courts & news. District-level crime maps.

🤝

Survivor Support

Anonymous reporting, FIR guidance, legal literacy and counselling referrals.

🔗

Multi-Stakeholder Coordination

Police, legal aid, NGOs, counsellors and media working as one coordinated network.

📣

Awareness & Prevention

Digital campaigns, school modules on consent, community workshops on allyship.

⚖️

Policy & Accountability

RTI resources, annual scorecards, district-level accountability for institutions.
