Referral Information

Key institutions and organisations offering support in Kerala

ℹ️ Contact details may change. Always verify with the official source or call 181 for up-to-date referrals.
🏥

One Stop Centres (Sakhi)

Integrated support for women affected by violence — medical, police, legal, psychological and temporary shelter services at one location. Present in all 14 districts.

Call: 181

📞

Mithra 181 Women Helpline

24×7 women's helpline operated by the Government of Kerala. Crisis intervention, information, referrals and emergency response for women in distress.

Call: 181

⚖️

KELSA (Kerala Legal Services Authority)

Free legal aid and advice for women, victims of crime and marginalised communities. District Legal Services Authorities present in every district.

Call: 15100

🏛️

Kerala State Women's Commission

Statutory body that takes up complaints related to atrocities and violations of women's rights. Can summon officials and recommend action.

Thiruvananthapuram: 0471-2322590

🧑‍⚕️

Women Protection Officers (WPO)

Appointed in every district under the Domestic Violence Act. They help file domestic incident reports, assist with protection orders and facilitate referrals.

Contact via Sakhi / 181

🏢

Dept. of Women & Child Development

Nodal government department overseeing welfare schemes, child protection, Anganwadi network and social justice initiatives.

Website: wcd.kerala.gov.in
