Analysis

Key Findings & Inferences

Overall Crime Trajectory

Kerala witnessed a sharp upward trend from 12,659 cases in 2020 to 18,887 in 2024 — a 49% increase over four years.

Peak Year: 2023

2023 saw the highest recorded burden at 18,980 cases, indicating systemic accumulation of unresolved structural factors.

Domestic Violence

Cruelty by husband declined from 30.8% (2021) to 23.9% (2024) — yet still represents nearly 1 in 4 crimes.

Dowry Deaths

Numerically low but persistent: 6 (2020), 9 (2021), 11 (2022), 8 (2023), 3 (2024). Five-year average of 7.4 per year.
